# Daily Energy Flow

**English:** Daily Energy Flow displays current power values in W from live power sensors, but calculates daily kWh, house consumption, self-sufficiency, PV self-consumption and variable grid import costs from existing daily energy sensors in kWh or Wh.

**Deutsch:** Daily Energy Flow zeigt aktuelle Leistungswerte in W aus Live-Leistungssensoren an. Tages-kWh, Hausverbrauch, Autarkie, PV-Eigenverbrauch und variable Netzbezugskosten werden aus vorhandenen Tages-Energie-Sensoren in kWh oder Wh berechnet.

Version 0.2.0: Daily kWh and percentage calculations now use existing energy sensors for solar production, grid import, grid export, battery charge and battery discharge. Live W sensors remain unchanged for current power display.
